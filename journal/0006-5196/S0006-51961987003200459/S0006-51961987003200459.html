Blumea - Biodiversity, Evolution And Biogeography of Plants 1987 32(2): 459-472

Additions to Horsfieldia (Myristicaceae) including four new species

    De Wilde, W J J O

    Abstract

    New facts about various of the hundred species recognized in the recent revision of the Asian-Malesian genus Horsfieldia (Myristicaceae), as well as the description of four new species in this genus (H. urceolata, H. coryandra, H. elongata, H. discolor) are presented.
